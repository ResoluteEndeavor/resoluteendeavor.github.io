Research and Development

Resolute Endeavor is actively conducting research, tests, and engineering on vehicle technologies.

What do you mean by "Vehicle Technologies"

It is no secret that recent increases at the gas pump, vehicle shortages, volatile automotive markets, and other economic struggles have had an adverse impact on the average consumer. These same events have motivated us into taking matters into our own hands to do our part in trying to improve the quality of life.

Alternative power sources such as Solar, Wind, Hydrogen, and even Magnetism are being tested for ways to be gainfully employed to make improvements within the average individuals automotive experience. The broad categories listed above are just the surface of what is already on the market and simply need to be refined and simplified so that their improvements and reach everyday consumers.
