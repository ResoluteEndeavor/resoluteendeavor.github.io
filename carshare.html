What is Carsharing?

Car Sharing is a method of car rental where the owners of the vehicles are often private individuals, and the carsharing facilitator is a separate entity from the owner.

What are the benefit of carsharing?

Carsharing allows the customer to hand pick a vehicle to fit his or her unique transportation needs. Carshare apps like Turo have made it easy for an individual to scroll through a list of vehicles available for their selected dates, make their selections, and add on additional commodities that the vehicle's host offers.
