Research and Development

Resolute Endeavor is actively conducting research, tests, and engineering on vehicle technologies.

What do you mean by "Vehicle Technologies"

It is no secret that recent increases at the gas pump, vehicle shortages, volatile automotive markets, and other economic struggles have had an adverse impact on the average consumer. These same events have motivated us to take matters into our own hands to do our part in trying to improve matters for all automotive lovers.

Alternative power sources such as solar, wind, hydrogen, and even magnetic propulsion are being tested for ways to be applied to our vehicles. Technology advancements will improve the customer experience when booking their automotive experience with us. The broad categories listed above possess unlimited potentials for the automotive industry when applied efficiently. We are excited to see what the future holds for our vehicles.
